Víctor Navarro-Fernández

(he/him)
v.navarro-fernandez@imperial.ac.uk

I am a Research Associate (Postdoc) in the Department of Mathematics at the Imperial College London, working in the group of Michele Coti Zelati. I received my PhD diploma at Universität Münster, where I studied under the supervision of Christian Seis. Previously I obtained both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at Universidad Complutense de Madrid.

I am mostly interested in the analysis of partial differential equations, random dynamics, and mathematical models of physically relevant phenomena. In particular, my research focuses on problems related to transport and diffusion of passive quantities, stability and mixing, and applications to fluid dynamics.

I am supported by EPSRC “Stable structures and chaotic dynamics in fluid flows”, PI Prof. Michele Coti Zelati.
