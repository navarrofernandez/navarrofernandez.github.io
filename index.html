Víctor Navarro-Fernández

(he/him)
vnavarro@uni-muenster.de

I am a PhD student in Mathematics in the Institut für Analysis und Numerik at Universität Münster under the supervision of Christian Seis. I am a Research Assistant in the group Analysis and PDEs.

I obtained both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at Universidad Complutense de Madrid, with final theses supervised by Mihaela Negreanu.

I am mostly interested in partial differential equations and mathematical models with methods from all kinds of analysis. My research concerns problems related to fluid dynamics, transport and diffusion of passive scalars, stability estimates and convergence of numerical schemes.

I am supported by the DFG grant 432402380 and by the Cluster of Excellence Mathematics Münster.
