Reading Seminars

Advanced Topics in Analysis and PDE at Universität Münster

This our research group's reading seminar which runs all year round. We study either a specific topic following a classical reference or read mathematical papers related to mixing and turbulence, transport and diffusion, or whatever looks interesting to (at least) one of us. Some papers are classics, some are brand new on the arXiv.

Some of the topics I presented include exponential mixing for random dynamical systems, Lyapunov exponents and the Furstenberg-Kesten Theorem, 2D Navier-Stokes equation with a measure as initial vorticity, universal mixers or stability for advection-diffusion equations on the DiPerna-Lions and the Ladýzhenskaya-Prodi-Serrin setting.
